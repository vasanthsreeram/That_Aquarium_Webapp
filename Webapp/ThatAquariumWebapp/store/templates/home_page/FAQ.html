As an Essential Service provider, That Aquarium continues to operate and serve you during this time. We will do so responsibly and with your well-being in mind.

Top Questions

Q: I want to shop on your online store. Is it safe to receive packages from That Aquarium?

As we keep a close watch on the global impact of COVID-19, we are committed to continue doing our part to safeguard customers who wish to shop on our online store: www.thataquarium.com
We want to assure you that we are doing everything we can with our delivery partners to follow recent government advisories on hygiene protocols during the preparation and delivery of your orders.

The following precautionary measures have been implemented:

1. Packing - our staffs follow measures in accordance with WHO and local health authority protocols i.e. temperature taking, washing of hands, social distancing measures and Travel History Declarations.

2. Delivery - our delivery partners are monitoring the health of their drivers closely and have instituted the following precautionary measures:

  • Daily temperature to be checked of all drivers

  • Drivers to carry personal protective equipment i.e. sanitiser, gloves and masks

Q: What is That Aquarium doing to safeguard the well-being of shoppers in stores?

Your health and safety is our #1 priority.

We have allocated alcohol-based hand rubs as well as hand soaps to all our outlets for your usage.

We ask that you keep a 1-metre separation from others when shopping in stores and while queuing for the cashier. We have marked out clear lines on our floors as a guide and ask for your cooperation in this matter.

Customers entering our stores would also be required to have their temperatures taken and hands sanitised. To protect you and other customers, we request that anyone with a temperature of more than 37.4°C refrain from entering the store.

Q: How do I place an order?

Shopping with us is easy! Simply follow the steps:

1. Visit www.thataquarium.com and browse our catalogue.
2. Add items to your cart by clicking “Add to cart“.
3. To review or edit your item quantities, click on the “Cart” icon located at the upper right corner of the page.
When you’re ready to place your order:
4. Click on “Checkout” and your order summary will appear for review.
5. If you have an applicable “Promo Code”, key it in and click “Apply”. Changes will take effect once you have successfully entered the code.
6. Fill in your preferred Shipping Method, Shipping Address, and Payment Option then click on “Place Order” to proceed with the payment.

You will receive a confirmation email shortly after you have successfully placed your order.

You can also drop by any That Aquarium outlets and our staff will be happy to help.

Q: Can you notify me when an “Out of Stock” product is back in store?

We love our products and know how frustrating it can get when a product is out of stock. Please click the “Notify Me” button located on the product page. You will receive an automated notification via email once the item is back in stock on thataquarium.com.

Return Policy

We aim to ensure that all products delivered to you arrive in perfect condition. However, should your parcel appear damaged at the time of delivery, please refuse the delivery and notify us here immediately.

If you are returning a product because we have sent the wrong item or the item is faulty/damaged, replacement is available within 14 days of delivery of the product, and delivery fee of the replacement product would be waived. In the event the product returned is not damaged, we may at our discretion decide not to replace, repair or refund you for the product and/or require you to pay all reasonable delivery and servicing costs.

Please note the following exceptions to our return and exchange policy:

  • Sale items are final and cannot be exchanged or returned.
  • Returned items must be in original packaging, with no visible signs of wear or use ie. Labels and seals intact. In the event that any labels and seals are removed or tampered with, we reserve the right to reject the return of the item.
  • Items purchased as part of a set or multi-item pack must be returned as a whole set. In the event the returned product is not a complete set, we reserve the right to reject the return of the item.
  • This return policy does not apply to items which have been stated as non-exchangeable or non-returnable.
  • For online orders, shipping costs associated with the return of the product will not be covered by us.
  • Due to the nature of customized products, sale of customised items are final and not eligible for return and exchange . As such, That Aquarium Pte Ltd is unable to accept returned products that were customized except in situations where the return is due to unforeseen damage such as water leakage. Hence, kindly check products thoroughly upon delivery, before signing the receipt of delivery.
